    The UpSet plot below shows the size of each FEATS, as well as sizes of subsets of terms enriched in only one FEA, and sizes of subsets of terms enriched in two or more FEAs.

    We refer to a subset of terms enriched in two or more FEAs as a functional enrichment analysis term-set intersection, or FEATSI.
